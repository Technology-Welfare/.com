About Our Foundation

Welfare Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to alleviating poverty, providing disaster relief, and supporting education initiatives over 5 contries. Since our founding in 2020, we've helped over 1 lakh people across 3 countries.

Our mission is to create sustainable solutions to global challenges by empowering communities and individuals through direct aid, education, and advocacy. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities and opportunities to thrive.
